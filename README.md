## ğŸ§  About the Workshop

Extended Reality (XR), including **VR**, **AR**, and **MR**, is reshaping how people interact, communicate, and collaborate.

Beyond mirroring real-world interaction, XR enables **social augmentation**: the intentional enhancement of social and emotional cues to improve understanding, engagement, and inclusion.

This workshop brings together researchers, designers, and practitioners to critically explore how social augmentation can be designed, evaluated, and deployed in ways that are **natural**, **ethical**, and **context-sensitive**.
